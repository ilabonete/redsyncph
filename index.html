About RedSync PH

RedSync PH is a digital health platform built for Filipino patients living with Hemophilia. It helps manage bleeding episodes, treatments, and medications with smart tracking, reminders, and dosage tools. With an integrated AI Chatbot, patients and caregivers can easily access guidance and support anytime. Our mission is to simplify hemophilia care and improve quality of life through technology.
